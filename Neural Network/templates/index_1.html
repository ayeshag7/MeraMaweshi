Rural and Agriculture Informatics Lab

Welcome to the Rural and Agriculture Informatics Lab (RAIL) at Namal College, Mianwali, Pakistan. This lab aims to provide easy to use solutions to the current problems of Agriculture and Livestock & Dairy sectors of Pakistan. In particular, we focus on mobile applications, web applications, machine learning algorithms and data analytic in e-agriculture. Team of young and enthusiast IT professional are working under the supervision of highly qualified professors to bring the digital revolution in the agriculture sector. Namal College is also collaborating with Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) and government departments to bring socio economic change in backward region of Pakistan.



Funded Project

Intelligent Disease Diagnosis System for Livestock in Rural Pakistan

An online disease diagnostic system for the livestock of rural areas' farmers. The project is confined to four types of animals commonly owned in rural areas: cows, buffaloes, goats and sheep. The product is capable of running as a stand-alone application on computers as well on android devices. The possible disease prediction is done through the selection of apparent unusual symptoms, found in the animal, on an easy to use localized interface.


  • Funding Agency: Ignite National Technology Fund
  • Grant: PKR 4.4 million
  • Duration: 12 months
  • Collaborators: Dr. Mian Muhammad Awais (LUMS), District Livestock Office, Mianwali
