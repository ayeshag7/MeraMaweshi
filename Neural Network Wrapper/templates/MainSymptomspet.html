About Us

Rural and Agriculture Informatics Lab (RAIL) at Namal University Mianwali is providing easy to use solutions to the problems of rural farmer communities of Pakistan.
Intelligent Disease Diagnosis System for Livestock in Rural Pakistan is one of the projects of RAIL, funded by Ignite, National Technology Fund, Government of Pakistan.
