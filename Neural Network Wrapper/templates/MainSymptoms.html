Respiratory System

  • Abnormal/wheezing/whistling lungs sounds

  • Cough

  • Discharge from nose

  • Dyspnea - Difficult or laboured breathing / shortness of breath / breathlessness

  • Eyes and nose discharge

  • Faulty Drenching - drenching performed in a wrong way

  • Respiratory Distress - Sharp pulling of the chest below and between the ribs with each breath. Grunting sounds

  • Sudden Death

  • Tachypnea - Rapid Breathing

  • Swollen_throat

About Us

Rural and Agriculture Informatics Lab (RAIL) at Namal University Mianwali is providing easy to use solutions to the problems of rural farmer communities of Pakistan.
Intelligent Disease Diagnosis System for Livestock in Rural Pakistan is one of the projects of RAIL, funded by Ignite, National Technology Fund, Government of Pakistan.
